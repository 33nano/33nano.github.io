Mater Artium Necessitas 🚀
@ Prince Namwali | Friday, Apr 16, 2021 | 9 minutes read | Update at Friday, Apr 16, 2021

Necessity is the mother of invention, but it all starts with a question. Is this the only way? What do i need that the currently available options don’t provide? How can we change this? Why? How come? Is there a better way? How can this be improved? What if? And the list of questions goes on and on.

Necessity is behind why we search for information on the internet. You could be looking for the definition of a word, a code solution on stack overflow, more information about a person or just about anything you are curious about. Necessity can be driven by curiosity, frustration, longing for something better, a new and improved solution or a remix of something exisiting. As a lifelong learner, i am drawn to a multitude of examples, but in this post i will only cover the ones what i find intriguing and explore some questions of my own.

  1. What problems does Game Development solve?
  • Being a game developer is one of those revered careers, but when i began to ponder i couldn’t find any reason why a person would become a game developer. Anyone can make a video game with out without code, heck the easist type of game to make is a visual novel and its basically the same as a powerpoint. Alright you spent a year or more to create your first ever indie game, but have you solved? If people keep using the same approach to making video games, i dont think… Observe the masses and do the opposite, but everyone wants to do the same thing as the massess. I believe the beauty of gamification is being wasted on creating addictive mobile games/ video games that contain no value. I believe using gamification as a means for things like Neuroscience, learning and real world problems is a step in the right path, but how many people are doing it?
  1. Is it possible to keep up with TV content without watching TV?

When i first had this question, i was aware of a service by the name VidaAngel, a service that enables consumers the ability to filter out scenes which you wish not to see in movies or any TV show. My astonishment with using Vidangel allowed me to question, what if? What if you could skip watching TV altogether and still be in the so called zone of awareness? This would save you precious time and the detrimental effects of blue light devouring your optic nerves. The bigger challenge is answering the how? Isnt human intervention ncessary? What are the odds of summaries being biased?…

  1. What would a virtual fitting room look like?
  • The good thing about shopping offline at a clothing store is that the consumer has the added advantage of a physical fitting room. In a fitting room the consumer can directly found out the appropriate clothing size and also the clothing appearance. With a physical fitting room you dont need to visualize the appearance of the clothing in your hand because you can make it a reality and decide whether or not your choice actually suites you. The disadvantage of shopping for clothes online is that there is a limit to ones visualization. Just because you think you would look cool wearing those clothes doenst gracefully translate in reality. Visualization is a problem and the need is a clear indicator, a mirror of sorts. This is were i propose a cool idea of a virtual fitting room. With the advancements in Augmented Reality (AR), Virtual Reality (VR), Mixed Reality (MR) and Extended Reality (XR), i believe its possible. Imagine for a second, creating a 3D model of yourself, think of it as your Gravatar, but optimized for shopping. This 3D Gravatar would function as your mirror, instead of the relying on the imperfect mirror of visualization. If the clothes are too big or too small the changes you see would be a direct reflection of that….
  1. Is it possible to use GDScript as a scripting language outside of the Godot Game Engine?

However, i believe GDScript as it is would be a great addition to applications that utilize other languages like Lua. GDScript would be great for adding gamification features to regular programs like Gimp, Aseprite, Inkscape and so many more…
  1. Is there a mind mapping program that follows the Tony Buzan Style?

  • The USA is a warzone of racial wars,income inequality, gun violence, gender inequality, expensive healthcare, but yet they boast of being the best country in the world (irony). Perhaps one the biggest problems in the USA is gun violence… The statistics of gun violence in the USA are wild with more than 30K deaths reported anually. I once thought about a radical solution while digesting yet another shooting incident headline that happened in the year 2015, Charleston, South Carolina… My radical proposal is for a way to track guns in real time, but thats absurd… What about ghost guns? How the heck do you even track guns? A metal detector with a wide range that can differetiate between regular metal and the specific metallic composition of a gun? You must be absurd? You are definitely living in a fantasy? Who are you to even write about guns? I am aware its a large topic, but the facts that its something you cant avoid means its a problem worth addressing…
